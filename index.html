01 — About

An unusual path to AI

I did not come from a computer science background. I studied Political Science, earned an O-Level diploma in computing, and spent a period preparing for civil services before recognising that building with technology was where I needed to be.

What followed was a year of focused, self-directed learning — Python, machine learning, NLP — with no bootcamp and no formal CS degree. Just building real projects, iterating, and learning from results.

That unconventional background is now my strongest differentiator. Most data professionals cannot think rigorously about governance, policy systems, and social impact. I can — because I have studied them formally. I work at the intersection that pure engineers rarely reach.

Currently pursuing a Master's in Political Science while building AI tools for governance and policy problems. Actively targeting Applied AI, Data Analysis, and AI Policy roles in India and Germany.
